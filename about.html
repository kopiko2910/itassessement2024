About ESSENTIA

ESSENTIA is a luxury perfume brand founded on the belief that every scent tells a story. We craft high-end fragrances using rare botanicals, timeless techniques, and modern innovation to bring out the essence of you.

Since our inception in 2020, we've aimed to blend tradition with sophistication. Our mission is to empower every individual to express themselves through the art of fragrance.

From concept to creation, every bottle is a journey, and every fragrance is a memory in the making.
